Session 1

Wavebreaker dives back in to the water and is given the riddle at the end of the cave. After returning to his friends and consulting with them, he realizes he might have the answer. Wavebreaker returns before the Dragon door, to speak the name Silence and door opened. He took the items. They left the cavern and emerged into the forest. Happened upon a bridge with roads leading East and West. To the East they saw plumes of black smoke and decided to investigate. Eldathine was visited my Eldath: The Savage God Malar has found a new master. The High Hunt is coming. Arriving at the caravan, they discover a scene of the macabre. Bodies of men thrown on a pyre, burning their flesh from bone. The smell of burning flesh was wafting through the air. Approaching the caravan, they realize that these men were burned alive, without being stripped of anything. It is then that they hear the call for help. Coming around the cart they happen upon a man, with only one working arm attempting to defend himself from a pack of wolves circling him, closing in for the kill. They take their surprise attack to attempt to slay the foul beasts, but in the end, it is Woeber's [[speak with animals]] that causes the beasts to depart. This saved Owic, if only momentarily, and he let them know all that they needed to know. Hely was taken, along with Malia, who is going to be sold into slavery. The group is at a crossroads on going to Luskan because Wex is hesitant about returning to his former city which he is banished from. Wex threw the drow poison darts into JimJars neck and put him out. That in turn caused Ront to rage. They killed Ront, and framed JimJar for his murder. Eldathine used Zone of Truth on Wex, and caused him to admit to his background in Luskan. Finished the session with the decision that the return to Luskan to save Hely is a must.
